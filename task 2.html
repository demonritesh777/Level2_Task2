Lata Mangeshkar

Indian singer and music composer

Biography

  • Born-September 28, 1929 Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India
  • Died - February 06, 2022 Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
  • Occupation- Singer and music composer

"Lata Mangeshkar was an Indian singer and music composer. Her contribution to the Indian music industry in a career spanning eight decades gained her honorific titles such as the "Queen of Melody" and "Nightangle of India". "
